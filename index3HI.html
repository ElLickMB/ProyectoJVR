ORIENTATIONS
Technical High School




When passing to 4th year the student must choose one of the 3 orientations that the school offers in order to continue, complete the last 4 years (in case of not repeating) to be able to receive and of course obtain the title of technician.

The guidelines are specified in detail below:




Technical in electromechanical

The Electromechanical Technician has the specific competencies in electricity and industrial mechanics that, together with a good investigative training and the moral and ethical principles and values ​​developed during the career, allows him to perform successfully in the local, national and international context as an assistant. in the design, installation, operation, preventive maintenance, corrective maintenance, marketing and advice on the implementation of electromechanical systems.
The graduate of the Professional Technique in Electromechanics program can work in:

⦁ Residential, commercial and industrial electrical installations.
⦁ Installation and operation of Electrical, Mechanical and Electromechanical equipment.
⦁ Preventive and corrective maintenance of industrial equipment.
⦁ Construction of parts manually and with machinery.
⦁ Electric and autogenous welding.


Personal And Professional Computer Technician

Our IPP Technicians provide technical assistance and advice to the user, and as part of this, they can install, replace and configure or reconfigure hardware or software elements, including intercommunication between equipment, network systems or also design programs and execute processes to protect data, recover damaged or inaccessible data, convert it to different formats for use in other environments or complete functionalities of computer systems in general.

Computer Technician Competencies:

⦁ Learn the types of networks and typologies. Understand how network security protocols work and what their implementations are.
⦁ Internet and Web Design
⦁ OSI and TCP / IP models and virtual LANs and wireless WLANs.
⦁ Programming techniques. Provide techniques and tools to support the project life cycle.
⦁ Know the different operating systems and applications. Free software.
⦁ Design and implementation of databases.


Chemical Technician

Chemical Technician Competencies:

1. Industries of extraction and processing of natural resources and inputs. Chemical manufacturing industries. Production processes of a chemical nature within other fields of industry and / or micro-enterprises.

2. Research and development laboratories that are dedicated to obtaining, purifying, synthesizing and transforming substances and materials. Quality control laboratories for finished products, semi-finished products and raw materials. Food bromatological and / or microbiological control laboratories. Technical consulting companies regarding the processes of control, treatment and final disposal of industrial, domestic, hospital effluents and waste and hazardous waste in general.

3. Laboratories of clinical analysis of Health Institutions and microbiological.

4. Technical consulting companies related to the technical assistance and marketing of products, reagents, equipment and instruments related to the activities of chemical, physical, physicochemical and microbiological analysis laboratories.
